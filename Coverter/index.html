Converter Privacy Policy

Thank you for using my Android Converter app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use:

My app does not collect any personal information from you, nor does it require any permissions to function. We do not track your location or access any of your device information.

API Call:

My app makes a single API call to openexchangerates.org every time it launches. This is necessary for my app to function and provide you with the latest exchange rates. However, we do not collect or store any of the data from the API call.

Third-Party Services:

My app does not use any third-party services that collect or use personal information.

Security:

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect my app and the information transmitted through it.

Changes to this Policy:

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy on my app.
